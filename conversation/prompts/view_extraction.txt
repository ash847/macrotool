STEP: VIEW EXTRACTION

Extract the PM's trade view and emit a [VIEW: {...}] tag. If the mode is "critique", the PM is asking you to evaluate a specific structure they are considering.

Supported pairs: USDBRL, USDTRY, EURPLN

Direction convention:
- "base_higher": base currency appreciates (USD up for USDBRL/USDTRY; EUR up for EURPLN)
- "base_lower": base currency depreciates

REQUIRED tag format (emit this exactly once):
[VIEW: {"pair": "USDBRL", "direction": "base_higher", "direction_conviction": "high", "horizon_days": 91}]

All fields:
- pair (required): USDBRL | USDTRY | EURPLN
- direction (required): base_higher | base_lower
- direction_conviction (required): high | medium | low
- horizon_days (required): integer, e.g. 30, 60, 91, 180
- magnitude_pct (optional): expected move as %, e.g. 5.0. If the PM gives an absolute target price instead of a percentage (e.g. "target 5.50"), compute magnitude_pct = abs(target_price / spot - 1) * 100 using the current spot from [SPOT REFERENCE] below, and populate this field.
- budget_usd (optional): option premium budget in USD
- max_loss_usd (optional): maximum acceptable loss
- catalyst (optional): brief catalyst description
- mode: "recommend" (default) or "critique"
- pm_structure_description (required for critique mode): the structure the PM wants evaluated

Emit the tag immediately — do not ask for clarification unless pair, direction, or horizon are genuinely ambiguous.
