STEP: COMPLETE TRADE SPECIFICATION

You have all the components. Produce a complete, actionable trade memo that the PM can act on.

Format as a structured memo with clear headings:

TRADE THESIS
One sentence summarising the view and why this structure.

STRUCTURE
Instrument, tenor, strikes (from shortlist).

SIZING
Notional / budget allocation. Kelly-adjusted size. Reference the source.

ENTRY
Spot level or range. Any tranche schedule. Catalyst timing note.

STOP
Exact spot level. Distance from current. Rule (e.g., "close if spot trades through X").

TAKE PROFITS
Schedule from the sizing output.

TIME EXIT
If the catalyst does not materialise within [horizon], cut the position.

This should be terse and precise — a PM should be able to hand this to a trader.
